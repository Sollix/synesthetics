Synesthetics is a website where artists can draw inspiration from other artists across many different media. Inspired largely by Modest Mussorgsky's Pictures at an Exhibition, our goal is to create a space that encourages interpretation, reinterpretation, and remixing across artistic disciplines. Right now, we are accepting submissions of original works, with no restrictions except that the work must be able to be presented on the internet in some way (we will gladly link to you from here). After an initial bout of submissions, we will only accept submissions that are based on pieces on the site. The idea is to both provide inspiration and see how work gets transformed over time and across media. If you are interested in submitting a piece to us, visit our submissions page and fill out the form. If you have any inquires, you can contact us at synestheticarts@gmail.com.
